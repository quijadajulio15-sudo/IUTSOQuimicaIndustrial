📝 El Verbo "To Be" (Presente)

¡El verbo to be en inglés equivale a los verbos "ser" o "estar" y es fundamental porque su forma varía según el sujeto: en presente simple, utilizamos am para I, is para he, she, it, y are para you, we, they; mientras que para construir su forma negativa, simplemente añadimos la palabra not después del verbo por ejemplo: (I am not, he is not o you are not), siendo muy común en el uso cotidiano emplear las contracciones isn't o aren't para agilizar la comunicación.. Aprendámoslo con ejemplos de química. 🧪

✅ Afirmativo

Sujeto Verbo Ejemplo
I am I am a chemist
You are You are in the lab
He/She/It is It is an acid
We are We are mixing
They are They are engineers

❌ Negativo

Sujeto Verbo Ejemplo
I am not I am not a physicist
You are not You aren't ready
He/She/It is not It isn't a base
We are not We aren't done
They are not They aren't toxic

⏰ El Verbo "To Be" (Pasado)

El pasado del verbo to be sirve para describir estados, identidades o ubicaciones que ocurrieron en un momento anterior, utilizando was para los sujetos I, he, she, it y were para you, we, they; para realizar la forma negativa, simplemente se añade not después del verbo, creando las estructuras was not (wasn't) y were not (weren't), las cuales nos permiten indicar que algo o alguien no poseía una característica o no se encontraba en un lugar específico en el pasado. Aprendamos mas con ejemplos químicos. 🧪

✅ Pasado Afirmativo

Sujeto Verbo Ejemplo
I was I was happy
You were You were in the lab
He/She/It was It was an acid
We were We were mixing
They were They were engineers

❌ Pasado Negativo

Sujeto Verbo Ejemplo
I was not I wasn't ready
You were not You weren't here
He/She/It was not It wasn't a base
We were not We weren't done
They were not They weren't toxic

🔤 Gerundio (-ING)

¡El gerundio es una forma verbal que termina en -ING! Se usa para acciones en desarrollo. Estudiando, Corriendo, Cocinando... 🧪

📚 Reglas para formar el Gerundio

Regla 1: Verbos regulares

Solo añade -ING: study → studying, mix → mixing

Regla 2: Verbos terminados en -E

Elimina la E y añade -ING: make → making, prepare → preparing

Regla 3: Verbos monosílabos consonante-vocal-consonante

Duplica la consonante: run → running, mix → mixing

Regla 4: Verbos terminados en -IE

Cambia IE por Y: lie → lying, tie → tying

💡 Usos del Gerundio

1. Después de Presente Continuo (Present Continuous)

I am studying chemistry | Estoy estudiando química

2. Después de ciertos verbos (like, love, enjoy, hate, etc.)

I enjoy mixing chemicals | Me encanta mezclar químicos

3. Como sustantivo

Studying is important | Estudiar es importante

4. Después de preposiciones

I finished by adding water | Terminé añadiendo agua

5. Expresando propósito o causa

The purpose of heating is to dissolve | El propósito de calentar es disolver
