📝 El Verbo "To Be"

¡El verbo "to be" es el verbo más importante en inglés! 🧪

✅ Afirmativo

Sujeto Verbo Ejemplo
I am I am a chemist
You are You are smart
He/She/It is It is acid
We are We are mixing
They are They are scientists

❌ Negativo

Sujeto Verbo Ejemplo
I am not I am not ready
You are not You aren't ready
He/She/It is not It isn't base
We are not We aren't done
They are not They aren't toxic
